User Experience and Design

Design, User, and Experience

This area of human-computer interaction examines the experience more over technology.
There has three different levels of user design, which are purpose, the method of interaction,
and the experience. User Experience Designers focus on the method of interaction.
They need to consider ease of use and aethetics.
The experience of the user must be made enjosyable to make the product appealing.
